Crew Rates is an invitation-only rate-sharing app for crew members on TV productions. Here's some of the things members can do:

Negotiate better rates using crowdsourced rate information

View a feed of rates for recent productions.





A blue badge next to a rate indicates a negotiating win. The number is the percentage increase between the offered rate and final negotiated rate.

Search for rates by show, company, network, job title, union status or genre.
When you filter by job title, you can view a rate summary chart. Drill down by filtering by date, union status, and genre.

Give back to the community

Use the Add a Rate form to anonymously share your own rates and help everyone negotiate better deals.
Add details to share your negotiating wins with the community.

Contact us to request an invitation.
