Crew Rates is an invitation-only rate-sharing app for crew members on TV productions. Here's some of the things members can do:

Negotiate better rates using crowdsourced rate information

View a feed of rates for recent productions.





A blue badge next to a rate indicates a negotiating win, with the percentage increase between the offered rate and final negotiated rate.

Search for rates by show, company, network, job title, union status or genre.
When you filter by job title, you can view a rate summary chart. Drill down by filtering by date, union status, and genre.

Give back to the community

Use the Add a Rate form to anonymously share your own rates and help everyone negotiate better deals.
Add more details in order to share your negotiating wins with the community.

How to sign up

Contact us to request an invitation.

How we use your data

In order to enable you to log in to the website and to communicate with you about your account, we collect and store your name and email address.

Any rate information you submit can be viewed by other users in an anonymous, aggregated format.

Once you've established your account, you can delete it at any time, which will permanently delete your personal information from our servers. Any anonymous rate information you may have submitted will be retained, but will not have any link to your identity.

Please read our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for more information.
