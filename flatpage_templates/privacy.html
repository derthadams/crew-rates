Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. It is crewrates.org's policy to respect your privacy and comply with any applicable law and regulation regarding any personal information we may collect about you through our website https://crewrates.org.

This policy is effective as of July 14, 2021 and was last updated on February 6, 2022.

Information We Collect

Information we collect includes any content you post to our website, as well as basic information you provide to us from your social media profile (such as name and email address), when you log in to our site using your social media provider.

Log Data

When you visit our website, our servers may automatically log the standard data provided by your web browser. It may include your device’s Internet Protocol (IP) address, your browser type and version, the time and date of your visit, other details about your visit, and technical details that occur in conjunction with any errors you may encounter.

Please be aware that while this information may not be personally identifying by itself, it may be possible to combine it with other data to personally identify individual persons.

Personal Information

We only collect and use your personal information when we have a legitimate reason for doing so. In which instance, we only collect personal information that is reasonably necessary to provide our services to you.

Security of Your Personal Information

When we collect and process personal information, and while we retain this information, we will protect it within commercially acceptable means to prevent loss and theft, as well as unauthorized access, disclosure, copying, use, or modification.

Although we will do our best to protect the personal information you provide to us, we advise that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and no one can guarantee absolute data security. We will comply with laws applicable to us in respect of any data breach.

You are responsible for selecting any password and its overall security strength, ensuring the security of your own information within the bounds of our services.

How Long We Keep Your Personal Information

We only keep your personal information for as long as you maintain an account on our website.

Deleting Your Data

If you would like us to delete your personal information, you can delete your account at any time by going to https://crewrates.org/settings, clicking "Delete Your Account", and following the instructions.
